# Chronology


**1858** Father William Barks is born (4 Mar.; d. 1940).

**1860** Mother Arminta Johnson is born (15 Mar.; d. 1916).

**1899** Brother Clyde Barks born (15 Aug.; d. 1980s).

**1901** Carl Barks is born at home on the Barks family ranch near Merrill, Oregon (27 Mar.).

**1908** Barks family moves to Midland, Oregon, to venture into the feed lot business; Carl attends one-room schoolhouse in the Lone Pine School District.

**1911–12** Lives in Santa Rosa, California, with parents and brother.

**1912–14** Family moves back to Midland.

**1914** Family moves back to the ranch near Merrill.

**1916** Leaves school after completing the eighth grade at age 15; mother dies.

**1917** Subscribes to Landon Correspondence School of Cartooning, which he abandons after a few lessons.

**1918–20** Moves to San Francisco in Dec. 1918; works in a print shop as an errand boy; tries unsuccessfully to sell cartoons to San Francisco newspapers.
