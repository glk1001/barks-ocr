**NW:** Do you remember the anti-comics hysteria in the 1950s—Dr. Fredrick Wertham and his book *Seduction of the Innocent*?

**CB:** Yeah. I agreed with a lot of what that guy said. (laughs) Although I've never seen the book or read it. **BH:** Many Barks fans are also E.C. fans. Isn't that an interesting contradiction because they're so totally different?

**CB:** Completely different—even the drawing style, the story construction, everything is almost exactly the opposite of the Disney ducks.

**NW:** Did you look at some of the E.C. comics?

**CB:** Oh, yeah.
