# 144 - September 1977 - 36 pages

Front cover: This cover — which shows Scrooge holding a huge cup while a monkey grinds an organ — is not by Barks, but is almost identical in conception to an unpublished cover by Barks that was submitted to Western on November 9, 1961. In that cover, listed as "six cups" in Barks's records, Scrooge has also reversed roles with an organ grinder's monkey, but is holding six cups, instead of one large cup.
