## UNCLE SCROOGE GOES TO DISNEYLAND (Dell)
### 1 - (August) 1957 - 100 pages
Untitled story - 20 - Scrooge tells Grandma of his days as the skipper of a steamboat, when he and Ratchet Gearloose, Gyro's grandfather, raced the Beagle family's steamboat to pick up a cargo of gold bound for New Orleans. *(Jan. 10, 1957)*
These pages are three panels deep.
This story is actually a segment of the story "Uncle Scrooge Goes to Disneyland," which takes up the entire book; the Barks segment begins on the third page, not counting the covers.
Barks's list shows this story as nineteen pages, but this may be an error; the story does not appear to have been stretched through the enlargement and rearrangement of panels.
Reprinted: Walt Disney Comics Digest No. 28, April 1971.
