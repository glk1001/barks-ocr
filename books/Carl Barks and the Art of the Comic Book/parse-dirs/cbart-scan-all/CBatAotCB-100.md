# THE BEST OF WALT DISNEY COMICS (Golden Press)

There were four titles in this set (the fourth, No. 96171, was made up of Floyd Gottfredson "Mickey Mouse" comic-strip reprints). They were published in a semi-hardcover format, with much better printing, color and paper than in ordinary comic books. Barks's authorship of the reprinted stories was acknowledged in brief introductions to each book.

## 96170 - 1974 - 52 pages

DONALD DUCK in Frozen Gold - 23 - Reprinted from Donald Duck Four Color No. 62, 1945.

Changes: The last three panels on the eleventh page and the first five panels on the twelfth page were deleted.

DONALD DUCK in Mystery of the Swamp - 23 - Reprinted from Donald Duck Four Color No. 62, 1945.

Changes: The third, fourth, seventh, and eighth panels on the sixteenth page and the first four panels on the seventeenth page were deleted.
