Reprinted: Uncle Scrooge No. 104, [April] 1973.
UNCLE SCROOGE - 1 (inside front cover) - Scrooge erects an "early American" office building — a tepee. *(Dec. 8, 1955)*
This page was apparently intended for Uncle Scrooge No. 14, June-Aug. 1956 (it was accepted on the same date as the other covers for that issue), but was replaced on the inside front cover by a page originally intended for Uncle Scrooge No. 11, Sept.-Nov. 1955.
UNCLE SCROOGE "A Cold Bargain" - 26¾ - Scrooge outbids the government of Brutopia for a frozen ball of Bombastium, the rarest element known to man. When Scrooge takes the ball to Antarctica to make sure that it will never melt, he is pursued by the Brutopians. They lose interest in the ball after learning that Bombastium is good only for making ice cream in huge quantities. *(Aug. 2, 1956)*
A circulation statement fills out the last page.
