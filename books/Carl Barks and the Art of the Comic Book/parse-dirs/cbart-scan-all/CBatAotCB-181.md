**206 (18/2) - November 1957 - 36 pages**

Front cover: Donald and the nephews are tangled in taffy. *(Jan. 31, 1957)*

DONALD DUCK - 10 - Scrooge lets Donald manage a run-down hotel, then shows up in disguise to see how Donald is doing. *(Jan. 17, 1957)*

Potato sacks are labeled "San Jacinto" and "Tulelake" (the California towns where Barks and his brother lived at the time).

Reprinted: Walt Disney's Comics No. 355, April 1970; No. 453, June 1978.
