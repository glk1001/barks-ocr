### UNCLE DONALD AND HIS NEPHEWS FAMILY FUN (Dell)

Front cover (pencil rough only): The ducks sit together, each doing what interests him; for example, the nephews work a puzzle and Scrooge "reads" a thousand-dollar bill. *(Apr. 25, 1960)*

Another artist made a painting for the cover, based on a pencil sketch by Barks. A surviving Barks sketch for this cover differs sharply from the published version. In Barks's drawing, the nephews are reading comics, Scrooge is "reading" a hundred-dollar bill, Donald is reading the sports pages (rather than "investment news"), Grandma is reading a cookbook (rather than an "album"), and Daisy is reading a newspaper with the heading "beauty" (rather than writing in her diary).
