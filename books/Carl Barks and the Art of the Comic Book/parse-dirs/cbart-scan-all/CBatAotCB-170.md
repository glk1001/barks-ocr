## 78 (7/6) - March 1947 - 52 pages

DONALD DUCK - 10 - Donald buys a safe to protect his valuables during a wave of burglaries, and to protect a jar of jam from the nephews. *(Oct. 28, 1946)*

On the cover of this issue a box is labeled "Bark's [sic] Jiffy Chicken Dinner" — a double joke, since Barks had a chicken farm at San Jacinto, California, in the nineteen-forties. The cover was drawn by Carl Buettner, who was for a time art editor at Western Publishing's West Coast office.
