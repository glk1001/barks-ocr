Barks's drawings on the covers of *Daisy and Donald* No. 25, *Uncle Scrooge* No. 144, and *Walt Disney's Comics* No. 405 were done after the above date, but no specific date is available. As mentioned in the bibliography entries for

those covers, the drawings were not done specifically for comic books.
